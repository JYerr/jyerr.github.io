Situation

The problem

Acquisition cost and portfolio losses were both trending up at once, which meant standard levers — tightening the box or spending more on the top of funnel — would only fix one at the expense of the other. The brief was to find segments where both could move in the same direction.

Approach

What I did

  • Profiled loss and CAC by acquisition channel and applicant segment to find where the two were misaligned.
  • Pruned top-of-funnel spend on segments with high CAC and no offsetting loss benefit.
  • Designed a risk-based gap funding strategy — approving more marginal applicants where a partial-funding structure kept expected loss in line with the existing book.
  • Piloted a refinance product alongside the policy change to widen the addressable segment without adding first-loss risk.
